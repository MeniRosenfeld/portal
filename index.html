Why Bitblu

Crypto-currency investment is hard. To invest smartly, you need to know the meaning of terms like Litecoin, PPCoin, Ripple, ASICMiner and --another asset--. We build simple indexes and investment tools that turns this complex world into something as easy to understand as S&P 500.

Why should you trust us?

We value our customers and see them as our partners, not (just) our source of revenue. We are gathering the best minds in the Bitcoin world to make sure your assets are secure and well-managed. We will set the bar on transparency in the Bitcoin world. It is important to us that you understand each component of your investment portfolio, and will be able to rest assured knowing how your funds are doing. We promise to never play dirty, and be completely honest and open about what do and how we do it.
